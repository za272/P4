NBA Playoffs News

Knicks Reach Eastern Conference Finals

The New York Knicks advanced after defeating the Milwaukee Bucks 4–2. Jalen Brunson continued his elite playoff run, averaging 34 points per game.

Pacers Upset the Celtics in Game 7

Indiana pulled off the biggest shock of the postseason by beating Boston behind Tyrese Haliburton’s 38-point performance.

Thunder Dominate the West

OKC overpowered the Timberwolves 4–1 to reach the NBA Finals. Shai Gilgeous-Alexander led the way with 32 PPG.

Nuggets Eliminated in Round 2

Denver fell in a tough 7-game battle to Minnesota, ending their bid for another championship run.

Lakers Knocked Out in First Round

The Lakers lost 4–1 to Denver despite LeBron James scoring 40 in Game 4.
